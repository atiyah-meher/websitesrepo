Humanize AI Text -

This AI-powered tool transforms cold, robotic AI-generated text
into warm, relatable, and authentic human-like prose, making your content come to life and resonate with your readers.


GPTzero -

GPTzero is a free and open-source AI tool that can detect text generated by large language models like ChatGPT, GPT-3, and Bard.
It is trained on a large corpus of human-written and AI-generated text and can detect AI on sentence, paragraph, and document levels.
GPTzero is a valuable tool for educators, researchers, journalists, and anyone who wants to ensure the authenticity of online content.

Simplified -

Simplified is an all-in-one AI tool that helps creators, marketers, and businesses of all sizes automate their tasks and workflows.
It offers a wide range of features, including AI Writer, AI Image Generator, Magic Resizer, Animation Maker, Video Editor, and Social Media Planner.


LightPDF -

LightPDF is a free and easy-to-use online PDF solution that offers a variety of tools
for editing, converting, compressing, merging, splitting, and managing PDF files.
It is a popular choice for students, professionals, and businesses of all sizes.
